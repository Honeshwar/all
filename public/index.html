About Us

Dhruv Research is an independent research firm that focuses on data-backed research on the current issues and trends shaping the country.

We conduct research on diverse fields such as economy, sports, politics, entertainment, etc. and aspire to be the brand of choice for the public and policy makers to make informed decisions across various fields.

If you want reliable and accurate information about the latest trends, you are at the right place!!

What We Do

We help you figure out what the nation thinks by collecting opinions of its people. We accomplish this through a 2-pronged strategy of having the Best Quality Data and the Best Techniques.

We build the Best Quality Data by combining the publicly available datasets across various fields with the opinions of our panel of people cutting across different regions and socio-economic strata of the country.

We work with cutting edge methodologies in the field of data science to arrive at the Best Techniques to derive useful insights from the datasets and opinions of the people in our panel for the public and the policymakers to make informed decisions.
